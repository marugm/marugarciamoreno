About


(b. 1986) Mariana García Moreno is an audiovisual designer currently based in Florianópolis, Brazil. She graduated from Universidad de Buenos Aires (Argentina) in 2010 and moved to Ireland in 2011 where she found a renewed passion for photography as she started exploring the world. Since then, she has traveled to 25 countries documenting different cultures, traditions, flavors and sounds through the lens of her camera. With an enormous curiosity for people and love for history, she hunts seconds of beauty and magic.

Mariana specialized in street photography and portraits, is a lomography-lover and is currently exploring the underwater photography world.

Based in Buenos Aires, Argentina.
