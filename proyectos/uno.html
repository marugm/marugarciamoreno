Makha Bucha


Makha Bucha day is celebrated the full moon of the third lunar month. Commemorating the day that, seven months after Buddha began his teachings, 1,250 monks gathered to hear Buddha preach. Buddha ordained these monks and spread the principles of Buddhism. This marked a key event in the development of the religion.

Photos taken in Chiang Mai, Thailand. February – 2014.
